# CTA and Microcopy Variants

This document lists call to action options and small bits of interface text that match the brand tone.

## Primary CTA Variants

- Talk to someone who understands
- Call for calm tech help
- Get steady help with your computer
- Speak with a patient expert

## Secondary CTA Variants

- Tell us what is happening
- Ask a question by email
- Describe your issue in your own words
- I am not sure what is wrong

## Form Microcopy

- "Describe what you are seeing or feeling when you use your computer. It is fine if you do not know the technical terms."
- "Share as much or as little detail as you are comfortable with. We will ask follow up questions if needed."
- "Include the best way and time to reach you so we can respond when it works for you."

## Error and Feedback Messages

- "Something went wrong sending your message. Please try again or call us at (603) 560-6630."
- "Thank you. Your message has been received. We will review it and get back to you as soon as we can."
- "This field is required so we know how to reach you."

## Accessibility Hints

- "If phone calls are difficult for you, let us know in the message and we can coordinate by email instead."
- "If reading long messages is tiring, it is fine to keep things brief. You can also ask us to call and talk it through."
